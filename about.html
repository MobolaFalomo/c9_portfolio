About Me

Software Engineer | Entrepreneur | Mum

I started learning about web development in 2022, I started with the ALX Software Engineering program at the time. It was my first hand experience with coding and it was amazing. To be honest it was the hardest thing I did in 2022. But the fascinating thing about my tech journey is the fact that I'm always learning something new and it keeps me fueled. ALX was so fast paced but I got to work with my team to build 2 projects, that was when I knew I wanted it all. The fact that I could feel my heart race each time I worked on a project was all I needed to know to keep building and improving my skills.

At some point in the program, I doubted myself and there were days I felt like I knew nothing and had no idea what I was doing, I had to find motivation myself. But I have come to discover that the easiest way to learn programming is to actually build projects. Here are some skills I learned so far;

  • Flask
  • DevOps
  • Python programming language
  • APIs
  • Team management

Learning As a Mum

Now, as much as I love taking care of my family, I love taking care of me too. And part of taking care of myself I have discovered is continuous hunger for knowledge. Learning while raising young children I must say is not for the faint hearted. And I love when my kids see me coding and are very interested, it's now a super power in our home as my first daughter is now learning to code with scratch. LOL!

So, I wanted to learn front-end development and so when the opportunity presented itself through HerTechTrail and I took it without second guessing it. And it has been so satisfying.

Learning with HerTechTrail has made my tech journey even better and smoother as the coaches teach way better and the classes are recorded so one ca go over the resources
